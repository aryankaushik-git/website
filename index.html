About Us

TecHacks, a student-run hackathon community, founded by the folks from ECE Department at Chitkara University , Punjab in 2019 with the motto to provide a quality experience where young minds collaborate together to innovate things for the upliftment of the society. TecHacks 4.0, as a in-person, Open-source hackathon, will give participants from various backgrounds the opportunity to engage with a wonderful community of hackers while also providing seminars and mentorship from industry leaders.
